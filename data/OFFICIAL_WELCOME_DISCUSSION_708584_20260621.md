## Full Welcome Message

We are excited to launch this competition in collaboration with Kaggle and The Pokémon Company. This is a great opportunity to develop innovative AI Training Agents that play the Pokémon Trading Card Game.

As a strategic game of its own, Pokémon TCG players must make gameplay decisions while being mindful of information they don't have (such as opponent's unknown hand and strategies).

In this competition, participants will train AI Training Agents that will compete against one another on the Kaggle platform. Agents will be evaluated on their ability to win matches against diverse opponents in a dynamic competitive environment.

### Key Aspects of the Competition

1. **Consideration of an opponent's unknown hand and strategies** — Agents must make decisions under uncertainty
2. **Simulation-based AI Training Agent training and evaluation** — Use the provided simulator for development and testing
3. **A combined Simulation Category and Strategy Category competition structure** — Encourages both performance excellence and strategic documentation
